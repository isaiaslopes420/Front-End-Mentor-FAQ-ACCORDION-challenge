FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium plans. The free plan gives you access to a large library of challenges, starter assets, and the community forum. The premium subscription unlocks Figma design files, premium challenges, and official solutions.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Definitely! You can use any project you build on Frontend Mentor in your portfolio. It’s an excellent way to demonstrate your real-world coding skills, attention to design detail, and ability to solve practical frontend problems to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

The best way to get help is by submitting your solution or asking questions in the Frontend Mentor community forum and Discord channel. The community is full of supportive developers who regularly review code and provide feedback on how to improve.
